School Church
Was established in 1933

St Michael's church is a parish of the Roman Catholic Church in Remuera Auckland. The same year the church opened it won the NZIA gold medal.

school library
Was built in 1980

In November 2010 the local councils formed to make the Auckland Council as a result of this process the seven public libraries in the region combined to make the Auckland libraries. These libraries include the North Shore library, the downtown libraries, the Manukau library, the Papakura library and Waitakere libraries

school auditorium
Was built in 2013

The school auditorium was opened in 2013. The Auditorium has seating for up to 1500, a large stage and a high quality multimedia system. Seating may be cleared to accommodate dining tables for up to 600 guests.
